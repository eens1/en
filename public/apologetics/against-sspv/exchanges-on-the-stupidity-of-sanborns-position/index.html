If you cannot see that their position is contradictory, then you have a big problem. Further, a cardinal by definition is a member of the Church. So, when they say that Bergoglio is a true Catholic cardinal, they are acknowledging him to be publicly Catholic (which is blasphemy). You should stop defending heretics (like Sanborn's non-Catholic group) and embrace the truth.
