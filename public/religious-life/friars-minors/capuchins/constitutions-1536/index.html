Constitutions of 1536

Source: capdox.capuchin.org

The subtitles come from the Rule, not the Constitutions.

  • Preface
  • Chapter 1
    • In the name of the Lord, begin the life of the Friars Minor.
  • Chapter 2
    • Of those who want to embrace this life, and how they should be received.
  • Chapter 3
    • Of the divine office, fasting and how the Brothers must go through the world
  • Chapter 4
    • May the Brothers receive no money.
  • Chapter 5
    • On the way of working.
  • Chapter 6
    • Let the Brothers not appropriate anything; let them ask for alms; and sick Brothers.
  • Chapter 7
    • Penance to be imposed on Brothers who have fallen into sin.
  • Chapter 8
    • Of the election of the General Minister of this Fraternity and of the Pentecost Chapter.
  • Chapter 9
    • Preachers.
  • Chapter 10
    • Of the admonition and correction of the Brothers.
  • Chapter 11
    • Let the Brothers not enter the monasteries of Nuns.
  • Chapter 12
    • Of those who go to the Saracens and other infidels.
